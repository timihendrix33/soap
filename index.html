Pitching is tough. On your brain. On your body. And on whatever’s left of your social life. So…for all those late nights, bad food and all the other fun stuff that comes par for the course, we’ve decided it’s only fair to acknowledge and reward those who willingly put up with it all.
Introducing the inaugural Son of a Pitch Awards, the first award that recognizes teams from Havas Creative Group who go above and beyond the call of duty.

The Gist of It

Pitching takes many forms and even the best efforts can sometimes go unrecognized. Each year, we’ll have a call for entries where any Havas Worldwide office can submit their best pitch story, in one of five categories described below, to win a $5k prize for the entire team:

5 Award Categories

Overcoming the Odds

We've all been there. David vs goliath. The OK Corral. Jack Bauer. Wil E. Coyote. This category acknowledges winning when the odds are stacked up against you. Kudos. You deserve more than a pat on the head and a dog biscuit. And we’re gonna give you more.

Breaking New Ground

It's the ‘ol catch 22. No entry without prior experience. Which means you need to create that experience. Out of thin air. In other words, yes- somewhere, at some point, someone at this agency has had prior experience on that sort of business. A little embellishment? Sure. After all, it’s advertising. Which is what we do best.

Most Creative Delivery

They don't call it strategic pitch theater for nothing. This category is for those producers and stage-theater managers who turn drama into winning. Big or small, no one really cares. As long as it contributes to a win. Or a kick-ass portfolio piece.

Most Networked

Networked. N-e-t-w-o-r-k-e-d. That’s a funny word. But this is no laughing matter. No, it’s the Son of a Pitch Awards. And sometimes, a pitch takes a village. Make that a Havas Village. And if you've networked more partners in the company than past agency presidents, this is the category for you.

The Win that Got Away

You should have won it. Everyone thought you should have won it. Hell, even the clients kinda/sorta mentioned you won it. But you didn't. Fortunately in this contest, even the losers go home winning. Tell us your story of the one that should have never gotten away, and we’ll give you 10 grand that should.
